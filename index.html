Our mission

We believe a good idea shouldn't die on a napkin. Benny Labs Foundation exists to help ordinary people turn creativity into invention — and invention into a better everyday life for their families and communities.

What we do

Three ways we move ideas forward

Every breakthrough starts with someone who refused to accept "that's just how it is." We help those people take the next step.

Spark invention

We encourage and mentor first-time inventors and makers — helping them shape a raw idea into something real, useful, and worth sharing.

Teach the skills

We support hands-on learning in creative problem-solving, design, and building — so more kids and adults gain the confidence to make things themselves.

Share what works

We help promising ideas reach the people they can help — putting practical inventions and knowledge into more hands, not fewer.

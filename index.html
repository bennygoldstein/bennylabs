Our mission

We exist to help ideas become real — inventing, developing, and proving new technologies, and sharing knowledge and opportunity so that progress benefits society as a whole.

Benny Labs Foundation conducts, supports, and funds innovation wherever it can move humanity forward — advancing concepts from an early spark all the way to a validated proof of concept, and promoting education and creativity for both adults and youth.

What we do

An engine for invention — and for the public good

We combine hands‑on R&D with education, so breakthroughs get built and the next generation learns to build them.

Invent & develop

We invent new ideas, technologies, and systems — and advance them from an early‑stage concept to a validated, working proof of concept.

Patent & protect

We conduct R&D and file patents to secure the intellectual property behind our work, so good ideas are protected, credited, and built to last.

License & scale

To reach the real world, we license or transfer proven technologies to partners positioned to manufacture, distribute, and bring innovations to people at scale.

Educate & inspire

We promote creativity for adults and youth — with educational materials, workshops, and grants and prizes that reward the next generation of inventors.
